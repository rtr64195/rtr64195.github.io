Rites and Practices Info

Since you are a new convert to the movement this page will briefly fill you in on some of the most critical things to know about Reese's Peanut Butter Cups. RPBC's, as they will be referred to from now on, are sold by The Hershey Company, which was created by, bless his name, H.B. Reese. RPBC's consist primarily of an outer chocolate shell confection molded into a circular shape and given divine and holy ridges on the outer perimeter due to the blessed Reese's wrapping paper. Inside of the heavenly body the seeker of revelation will find the truth about all reality in the form of a very sugary peanut butter filling.

There are three main tenets of this group.

  1. RPBC's must constitute 75% of a member's total caloric intake.
  2. Weekly worship sessions where ceremonial RPBC's are imbibed are required.
  3. The member must always wear RPBC themed attire.

RPBC's can come in many varieties, and this has caused some schism and separation amongst the various sects of cup worshippers. Scholars agree, however, that there historically have been three main divisions attempting to gain hegemony over the others: The Big Cups with Reese's Pieces, the Minis, and of course, the Originalists. This sect endorses the Originalist position, and as such any consumption of non-orthodox RPBC varieties will result in execution. However, our members are devoted, and so this is rarely necessary.
